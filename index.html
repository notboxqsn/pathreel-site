DISCOVER & COLLECT

Every place you've been — recognized.

PathReel cross-references your trips with UNESCO World Heritage Sites, US National Parks, Wikivoyage entries, and Geoapify's points-of-interest database. When you're near an attraction, it lights up. Tap to read about it, or check in to collect a verified stamp.

  • ✓Auto check-in within 200 m — PathReel notices you visited.
  • ✓Manual check-in within 500 m — for places that need a deliberate visit.
  • ✓Stamp collection — every check-in earns a passport-style stamp tied to the exact GPS coordinate and date.
  • ✓Wikipedia / UNESCO context — tap any attraction marker for an offline-cached info card.

SET IT & FORGET IT

Auto-record. No buttons.

Mark your home (or any anchor) as a geofence. PathReel uses iOS-native region monitoring to start recording the moment you step out and stop the moment you return — even with the screen locked, even after a reboot. Battery-conscious, restart-resilient.

Hybrid activity detection means walking, running, cycling, and driving are auto-classified within the same trip — switch from car to foot mid-journey and PathReel notices.
