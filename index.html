Documentation

Getting Started

Permissions

PathReel requires two permissions to work properly:

  • Location (Always Allow) — Required for background GPS recording.
  • Photo Library (Full Access) — Required to match your photos to routes and save exported videos.

Recording a Trip

Select your activity type and tap "Start Recording". PathReel tracks your route in the background. Lock your phone and use other apps normally.

Replaying a Route

Go to Trips > select a trip > tap "Replay Route". Control playback speed, camera height, and map style.

Adding Notes

Long-press any item in the timeline to add a personal note. Notes appear during replay and in exported videos.

Exporting Video

Tap the export button during replay. PathReel captures every frame and encodes it as HEVC video.

Insights

View driving statistics, records, and a heatmap. Filter by 7 days, 30 days, or all time.

Settings

Customize appearance (Day/Night), language (16 languages), and replay timing.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

PathReel is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

PathReel does not collect: personal information, location data to any server, photos, or usage analytics.

On-Device Storage

All data is stored locally on your device: GPS track points, trip metadata, notes, and app settings.

Permissions

Location (Always) — Used solely to record GPS routes. Never leaves your device.
Photo Library — Used to display trip photos and save exported videos.

Third-Party Services

Apple Maps/MapKit — Map display and POI lookup via Apple's servers.
App Store — Update check via iTunes API. No personal data sent.

Data Sharing

When you share via the iOS share sheet, PathReel provides the file to your chosen platform. PathReel itself does not upload anything.

Children's Privacy

PathReel does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
