Getting started.

§ 01PERMISSIONS

Always-Allow location + Full Photos.

Required for background GPS recording and photo-to-route matching. PathReel cannot work in "While Using" mode — recordings will pause when the app backgrounds.

§ 02RECORDING

Start, lock, walk, stop.

Pick transport mode (car, bike, walk, etc.) → tap Start. Lock the phone, switch apps freely. Distance updates every 3 m. Tap Stop when done — auto check-in scans within 200 m of saved targets.

§ 03AUTO RECORD

Geofence around home.

Set home location and radius (10–500 m). PathReel auto-starts when you leave, auto-stops when you return for ≥ 3 min. Notifications on iPhone + Apple Watch.

§ 04REPLAY

3D fly-through with controls.

Cinematic camera follows the route. Photos zoom in. Stops show place name and duration. Speed (1×/2×/3×), camera height (0.5×–3×), and map style (satellite/standard) all adjustable.

§ 05EXPORT

HD video + auto caption.

Replay → tap export. Generates HD video with all photos, stops, notes, and an auto caption (city, weather, season, places). 9:16 for vertical, 16:9 for YouTube.

§ 06BACKUP

iCloud + .pathreel files.

Auto-backup to iCloud Drive after each trip. New iPhone? Restore is automatic. Or export the whole library as a single .pathreel file via the share sheet.

Permissions

PathReel requires two permissions to work properly:

  • Location (Always Allow) — Required for background GPS recording. PathReel uses high-accuracy GPS to track your route even when the screen is locked or you switch to other apps. Go to Settings > PathReel > Location > Always.
  • Photo Library (Full Access) — Required to match your photos to routes and save exported videos.

Recording a Trip

On the Record tab, tap "Start Recording". Pick a transport mode (car, motorcycle, bike, walk, run, boat, or train). PathReel begins tracking with high-accuracy GPS, updated every 3 metres.

  • Lock the phone, switch apps, or pocket it — recording continues.
  • The map follows your direction of travel — forward direction always points up.
  • Distance, duration, and point count update in real time.
  • The transport icon switches automatically — if driving speed drops below 8 km/h a walking icon appears, and back when speed picks up.
  • Photos taken along the way appear on the route during replay.
  • Tap "Stop Recording" and confirm. PathReel scans every track point against saved check-in targets within 200 m.

Trip Detail

The Trips tab lists every recording. Tap a trip to see:

  • Full route on a satellite or standard map.
  • Timeline: start, photos, stops, end.
  • Tap any photo for full-screen preview.
  • Long-press a timeline item to add a note. Notes appear in replay and exports.
  • Long-press a trip card to edit its name, description, or delete (biometric required).

Replay

From the trip detail page, tap "Replay Route". The cinematic 3D flyover begins:

  • Camera follows the route in real time, pitch and heading matching travel direction.
  • Photos zoom in automatically when the route passes nearby — image, address, POI name.
  • Stops are highlighted with place name, address, and duration.
  • Compass top-right; clock, distance, speed top-left.
  • Speed control — 1×/2×/3×. All points render at every speed; only frame interval changes.
  • Camera height — 0.5×–3×. Higher shows more area, lower more detail.
  • Map style — toggle satellite / standard.
  • Final 5-second overview zooms out to the full route.

Exporting Video

During replay, tap the export button. PathReel re-runs the route and captures every frame as a high-quality HD video.

  • Copy & Share — Auto-generated caption (city name, weather, temperature, places, season, time of day) is copied; the system share sheet opens with the video.
  • Share Video Only — Share sheet directly, no caption.

The caption is built from real trip data — start/end city via reverse geocoding, weather from Open-Meteo, notable stops, and more. A different template is randomly selected each time.

Trip Groups

Group multiple trips for multi-day vacations or themed collections.

  • Trips tab → tap "Trip Groups" → "+ New Group". Enter name (required) + optional note.
  • Tap a group, use "Add Trips" for multi-select.
  • A trip can belong to multiple groups. Deleting a group does not delete the trips.
  • Long-press a trip inside a group to remove it. Long-press the group card to edit/delete.
  • Group Playback Duration — each group has its own duration (default 60 s). Tap to change (1–300 s).
  • Tap "Replay Route" to play all trips in sequence with smooth zoom-out / zoom-in transitions between each.

Insights

The Insights tab shows your travel statistics:

  • Total distance, driving time, average speed.
  • Records: longest trip, top speed.
  • Heatmap of every place you've been — tap to explore.
  • Filter by 7 days, 30 days, or all time.

Attraction Discovery

The Attractions tab searches nearby points of interest from multiple data sources:

  • Sources — UNESCO World Heritage, Wikivoyage, US NPS, Geoapify. Results deduplicated and merged.
  • Search center — current GPS, long-press the map, or search a city/address.
  • Search radius — slider, 1–50 km.
  • Read More — Wikipedia in your language via Wikidata language links; otherwise a multilingual description is generated. Images from Wikidata or Wikipedia geosearch.
  • Text-to-Speech — "Listen" picks the best voice (Siri > Enhanced > Default), detects text language.
  • Add to Check-in — ⭐ saves an attraction as a target.
  • Custom Places — add your own by name and address, or long-press the check-in map. Categories (café, restaurant, friend, historic, memorial, other) and notes.

Check-in Stamps

Switch to "My Check-ins" to manage your travel passport:

  • Stats cards — UNESCO, Featured (Wikivoyage), Local (Geoapify), My Places. Tap a card to filter.
  • Map — visited targets in tier colors with ✅; unvisited in gray. Tap a marker for the 200 m auto + 500 m manual rings.
  • Manual check-in — disabled outside 500 m, green within range. Tapping triggers GPS verification.
  • Auto check-in — when a trip stops, PathReel scans the track points and marks any target within 200 m as visited.
  • Detail — visited record shows mini map (attraction pin + your check-in spot), distance, method (Auto / Manual), timestamp, tier, description, notes, nearby photos (within 1 km, ±1 hr).
  • Delete — long-press (2 s) an unvisited target. Visited records are locked.

Settings

Appearance

  • Day / Night — every screen, modal, map, and exported video follows the theme.
  • Language — 16 languages. Detects your device language on first launch. UI, descriptions, TTS voice, and share captions all adapt.

Replay

  • Photo Display — 0.5–10 s, default 1.5 s.
  • Stop Display — 0.5–10 s, default 2 s.
  • Stop Min Time — 1–60 min, default 10 min. Filters out traffic lights, brief breaks.
  • Trip Duration — 5–300 s, default 30 s. PathReel distributes points evenly.

Auto Record (iOS)

  • Master toggle — iOS region monitoring around your home; works in background or terminated state.
  • Home Location — geocoded address; map confirms with the geofence circle.
  • Geofence Radius — 10–500 m, default 50 m.
  • Default Mode — transport used when auto-record starts.
  • Min Stay to Stop — 1–10 min, default 3 min.
  • Notifications — local notification on iPhone + Apple Watch in your language.
  • Invalid trip cleanup — auto-recorded trips shorter than the geofence radius are deleted with a notification.

Backup

  • iCloud Auto Backup — every trip auto-backed up; new iPhone restores automatically.
  • Export All — single .pathreel file via share sheet.
  • Import — duplicates skipped automatically.

Tips

  • Keep "Always Allow" location for best accuracy. "While Using" stops recording in background.
  • Take photos during the trip — they appear at the right place during replay.
  • Satellite map is the most cinematic for replays.
  • Export 9:16 for vertical platforms, 16:9 for YouTube.
  • Save attractions as targets before visiting a new city — PathReel auto-stamps as you travel.
  • Enable Auto Record for daily commutes — every trip tracked without manual taps.
  • All data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
