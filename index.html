Getting Started

Permissions

PathReel requires two permissions to work properly:

  • Location (Always Allow) — Required for background GPS recording. Go to Settings > PathReel > Location > Always.
  • Photo Library (Full Access) — Required to match your photos to routes and save exported videos.

Recording a Trip

Select your activity type (Hybrid, Walk, Run, Bike, etc.) and tap "Start Recording". PathReel tracks your route in the background. You can lock your phone and use other apps normally. When you're done, open PathReel and tap "Stop Recording".

Replaying a Route

Go to Trips > select a trip > tap "Replay Route". Control playback with speed (0.5x–3x), camera height, and satellite/map toggle. Photos and stops appear automatically during playback.

Adding Notes

In the trip detail view, long-press any item in the timeline (start, photo, stop, end) to add a personal note. Notes are saved permanently and appear during replay and in exported videos.

Exporting Video

During replay, tap the export button (box with arrow). PathReel captures every frame including photos, stops, and zoom-ins, then encodes it as HEVC video. After saving, you can share directly to any social platform.

Insights

The Insights tab shows your driving statistics (distance, trips, speed), records (longest trip, top speed), and a heatmap of everywhere you've been. Filter by 7 days, 30 days, or all time.

Settings

Customize appearance (Day/Night mode), language (16 languages supported), and replay timing (photo display duration, stop display duration).

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

PathReel is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

PathReel does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data to any server
  • Photos or media files
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

On-Device Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite:

  • GPS track points (latitude, longitude, altitude, speed, timestamp)
  • Trip metadata (start time, end time, distance, activity type)
  • Notes and descriptions you add to trips
  • App settings (language, theme, replay preferences)

Permissions

  • Location (Always) — Used solely to record GPS routes. Location data never leaves your device.
  • Photo Library — Used to read geotagged photos for display on routes, and to save exported videos. PathReel does not modify or delete your photos.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Maps / MapKit — Used for map display, reverse geocoding, and POI lookup. These requests go through Apple's servers per Apple's privacy policy.
  • App Store Update Check — PathReel checks Apple's iTunes API to notify you of updates. No personal data is sent.

Data Sharing

When you export a video or share content via the iOS share sheet, PathReel provides the file to the platform you choose. PathReel itself does not upload anything.

Children's Privacy

PathReel does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
